Hey, I am Katlego Seiphemo

life as a web developer...

My passion for web development was ignited in the unlikeliest of places - a township where opportunities were scarce. Growing up, I never imagined that I would have the chance to pursue a career in tech, let alone become a web developer. But fate had other plans. I stumbled upon platforms like Umuzi, Coursera, and SheCodes, which provided me with the fundamental access to coding that I so desperately craved. These platforms opened doors to a whole new world of possibilities, and I was determined to seize every opportunity that came my way. As I delved deeper into the world of web development, I discovered a sense of purpose and drive that I had never experienced before. I was fascinated by the endless possibilities that coding offered - the ability to create something from scratch, to solve real-world problems, and to make a meaningful impact on people's lives. Despite the many challenges that I faced along the way, my passion for web development only grew stronger. I was determined to succeed, not just for myself, but for all the others who had been marginalized and excluded from the tech industry. I knew that I had a responsibility to use my skills to make a positive difference in my community, and to pave the way for others to follow in my footsteps. Today, I am proud to say that I am a web developer, and that I have been able to turn my passion into a career. It's been a journey marked by hard work, perseverance, and a refusal to give up on my dreams. And I'm just getting started
